About Me

Name: Kirstin Lathrop

Date of Birth: January 4, 1991

Hi there! I'm Kirstin — a passionate and creative soul with a big dream: to become a game designer. I've always been drawn to storytelling, world-building, and crafting experiences that make people feel something real. I love creating, writing, and bringing ideas to life.

In my free time, you'll find me whipping up something delicious in the kitchen, screaming my lungs out on roller coasters at theme parks, or cozying up with movies and games. I'm a lover of all things fun, flavorful, and full of life.

I'm also a proud trans woman, and I celebrate August 8, 2015, as the day I began living authentically. Being trans is part of who I am, but it doesn't define me. I'm so much more — and in these dark times, I stay strong for those who can't. I care deeply about freedom, justice, and uplifting others — especially within the trans community.

Above all else, I believe in kindness, connection, and creativity. Thanks for taking the time to learn a little about me — I hope to use my journey, skills, and heart to make the world a better place, one story at a time.
